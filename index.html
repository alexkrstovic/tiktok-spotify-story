TikTok buzz and Spotify streams: related, but loosely

Each dot is a song. The further right, the more TikTok posts it generated. The higher up, the more it streamed on Spotify. The trend line shows a weak positive relationship — more TikTok activity does tend to mean more streams. But the massive spread tells the real story: plenty of songs went viral on TikTok and barely moved on Spotify, and vice versa. Expand the chart to explore them.

Discovering the Trends

TikTok helps — until it doesn't

Songs with no TikTok presence average 222M streams. That climbs steadily through Low (362M), Medium (615M), and peaks at High activity (658M) — nearly 3× more than songs with no TikTok at all. But here's the twist: truly viral songs (10M+ posts) actually drop back to 388M. Going viral on TikTok doesn't guarantee Spotify success — it may just mean the moment burned fast.
